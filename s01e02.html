barreling down on where Richard is sitting with his back to

Mike is bouncing on the roof of the box trying to reach the
control panel. The panel reads "Turn off hover mode?

position and dives behind one of the crates.

panel. It's just out of reach as he slips and nearly falls
off the edge. Finally he manages to press the "OK" button
and the box drops into the snow. 
 
The sudden loss of velocity sends Mike flailing off the
battery into the snow in front of it. It's incredible
momentum allows it to plow through another 20 feet of snow,
carrying Mike and the growing pile of snow and ice in front
of it right up to the base of the box Richard was sitting
on.
